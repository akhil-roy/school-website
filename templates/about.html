Dr. Ulhas Patil English Medium School

The Journey Of Dr. Ulhas Patil English Medium School, Savda Began In 2001 Under The Inspiration Of Our Respected Godavari Aai Along With The Far-sighted Vision Of Our Chairman Honourable Dr. Ulhas Pail Who Has Constantly Nurtured This School Under His Dynamic Leadership And An Overwhelming Personality.

Dr. Ulhas Patil English Medium School Is Part Of Godavari Foundaiton Which Was Established With The Aim Of Promoting Quality Education To The Students. The School Focuses On Holistic Development Of The Children By Engaging Them In Active Learning, Ensuring Commitment.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading school, nurturing the students of noble rank traditions, culture and democratic ideals to contribute Toactively in homes and families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide every student an ideal learning environment of intellectual rigour and high academic standards for the holistic and integrated development of our students. We promise to do the almost possible efforts to equipour students for lifelong challenges and opportunity.

Our Objectives

  • To impart progressive and quality education suited to the changing, needs and aspirations of our time.

  • To encourage the students to analyse and evaluate their experienceg learn to doubt, to question, to investigate' and to think independently.

  • To inculcate values in students which will make them critically aware of the social realities.

  • To develop children's character and personality through persona] guidance.

  • To develop concern for justice, human rights and commitment to the poor and the needy.

  • To bring about refinement of manners, gentleness in language and to develop habit of obedience, order and neatness in person and cleanliness in dress.

  • To contribute to educational thinking and innovative approaches in the field of education.

  • To impart truthful love of god, just and selfless love of fellowmen and kindness toall beings.

  • To impart knowledge and love to the ecological system and to develop dedicated concern to preserve the ecosystem.

  • To develop aesthetic sense and talents through co-scholastic programme.
