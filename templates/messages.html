Chairman's Message

The essential purpose of any educational institution is to deliver a learning atmosphere which brings out the best in the students. At DUPEMS, we make available a learning atmosphere that enthuses and inspires children, and encourages them to discover immeasurable potentials in their pursuit for knowledge. Since inception, our predominant goal has been to make DUPEMS a happy school, where teaching is a pleasure and learning is a joy; where learning and excelling is a passion, and beliefs and values have a dominant place.

DUPEMS believe that every child is gifted with an inimitable set of qualities and aptitudes, which need to be fostered so that they bloom. Therefore, every child deserves an education that nurtures academic excellence and all-round development, and ensures their welfare. We are inspired by these doctrines in everything that we do at DUPEMS.

The caring and devoted teachers are our pillars of strength. They teach not just with their minds but with their hearts. They make learning enjoyable and rewarding. They inculcate moral, ethical and cultural values in children that would make them good human beings. The educational programmes provided at DUPEMS are excellent and facilitates children's holistic development. Our accomplishments over the past 28 years since our inception in 2001 and our reputation as one of the best schools epitomise the success of our educational model. We are a school with an Indian mind, an Indian heart and an Indian soul. All these inspire us to continue to do our best, in preparing children for the 21st century.

I cordially invite you to explore DUPEMS as we re-dedicate ourselves every day, to impart the very best education to young minds.

Principal's Message

Dr.Ulhas Patil English Medium Shool Savda, since its inception, remains to be an extraordinary institution with one common purpose: building and empowering individuals to succeed. At DUPEMS, we provides a uniquely inclusive education that goes beyond grades to develop all-round academic excellence by building resilient minds and encouraging positive, multicultural perspectives. Our motive is to equip our students with confidence and resourcefulness that prepares them to grow, thrive and find their success in a fast-changing world.

DUPEMS vows to continually strive and instil an intrinsic desire in the students as to always strive to meet the high expectations they have set for themselves no matter what their goals are. Our school is envisioned to offer an all-inclusive,learnin e individual excellence that will inspire our students to turn into lifelong learners. I am looking forward to another amazing year ahead.
